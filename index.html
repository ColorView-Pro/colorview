Hello to everyone who opened this to know about us, We are 2 normal students, The first is one is Nour Eldeen and The second one is Ahmed Sameh, We have been thinking about making a project that can help the environment and the community, something that is genuinely rare to find, and no one still made it, We researched and found the perfect idea. Making a website to help color blind people, First we started by choosing which programming language will we choose and we found the perfect one and it was HTML because we were making a website so we are obviously going to choose it. We started coding with our knowledge and we researched and we kept reading articles on the diseases that color blind people face and what are the scientific names for every disease, then we started getting information about it, Then I kept thinking the whole time and such a good idea came in my mind, adding a color panel will make it even better. The fact that you can choose any color you want with HEX and RGB and HSL Sliders. You upload the image you have 2 useful things, If you are a person and you are trying to see what a color blind person sees that's helpful, It makes you be grateful for what you have and always thank god no matter what, Now the second useful thing and most important as a colorblind person we are trying to make you see the image that you choosed as close as possible as the original image. Not only that but we also added that you get to see the recent colors that you choosed the last time you logged in and you can select your favorite colors, It was such a hard progress to code all of this (Color Panel, Improving the UI for mobile users) so we used the help of chatgpt and gemini, They helped us improve our coding skills and English skills too. It's such an advantage for us and this era. We improved the ui and added english in the language, We also researched about the diseases like Tritanopia and Protanopia.. etc. Basically when you select the disease that you have in your eyes it gives you information about it. We also added a good UI for Mobile users, We added dark mode AKA gold mode. All of this was done in 5 days, But we stayed up and worked hard, woke up early. We even fought but we still understood eachother because teamwork and working hard is the key for success. I hope you like our project, More updates and features are coming soon. Yours Nour Eldeen & Ahmed Sameh.
